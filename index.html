Leona Lassak

I'm a 4th year Ph.D. student at Ruhr University Bochum in Germany. My research covers computer security and human-computer interaction (HCI), specializing in passwordless authentication and privacy. As part of the interdisciplinary research program SecHuman, I closely collaborate with legal scholars in projects concerning data privacy regulations.
